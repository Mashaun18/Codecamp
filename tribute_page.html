Dr Olusola Saraki

The Nigerian for All Nigerians.

Waziri of the Ilorin Emirate
  • 1933 - Born on May 17 in Ilorin, Northern Nigerian, British Nigeria

  • 1942 - Olusola Saraki was educated at Eko Boys High School. He attended the University of London, and St George's Hospital Medical School, London. He worked as a medical officer at the General Hospital, Lagos and the Creek Hospital, Lagos.

  • 1964 - Olusola Saraki came into politics when he contested in the 1964 parliamentary election for Ilorin as an independent, but lost. After the election, he returned to his medical practice in Lagos, only returning to party-politics in 1978/79

  • 1977 - In 1977, Olusola Saraki was elected as a member of the Constituent Assembly that produced the 1979 constitution. In 1979 he was elected a Senator of the Second Republic, and became Senate Leader.

  • 2012 - Olusola Sakari died on Wednesday 14 November 2012 in Lagos at the age of 79 after a losing a battle with cancer. He was laid to rest in Ilorin.
